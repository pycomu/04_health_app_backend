Mission and Vison

"Our goal is to motivate the next generation to live a healthy lifestyle. Assist young parents in understanding the nutritional needs of their children and provide opportunities for young brains to learn about their development. Know what you're putting into your body. Here are some healthy dishes to spice up your diet. More insight with minimal data."

Our children are healthy with a BMI under the threshold. We monitor their weight with the Happy Kids application, which provides nutrition advice and guidelines to maintain a healthy weight through its user-friendly interface. Is your child overweight? Simply enter your child's height and weight; the app will find out what BMI is recommended. The app helps you determine your child's calorie intake using a simple formula and recommends meals that are filled with essential nutrients. The app makes it easy for parents to track their children's growth. It can generate charts of historical information for you to see at a glance: average weight or height, changes in BMI.
